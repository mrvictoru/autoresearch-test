Project Pain Points項目痛點

1.Fragmented information: ticketing, supplier, transport, and demand data live in different places.資訊碎片化：訂單、供應商、運輸與需求資料分散在不同系統，難以整合。
2.Waste and stockouts: restaurants lose money when ingredients expire or run out at peak hours.浪費與缺貨：食材過期或尖峰時段缺料，都會直接傷害餐廳營收。
3.Manual planning is slow: staff must balance demand, shelf life, storage limits, and replenishment timing by hand.人工規劃太慢：員工要手動同時兼顧需求、保存期限、倉儲上限和補貨時機。
4.Bottom-line pressure: the system must increase service while lowering waste, labor effort, and holding cost.直接影響獲利：系統必須在提升服務率的同時，降低浪費、人力負擔和持有成本。
The goal is to turn restaurant inventory into a repeatable AI workflow that improves profit instead of relying on guesswork. 目標是把餐廳庫存管理變成可重複的 AI 工作流程，讓系統靠數據提升利潤，而不是憑感覺下決定。

Target Audience目標受眾

  • Primary market: restaurants, cafés, and multi-branch food operators that need tighter inventory control.主要市場：需要更精準庫存控制的餐廳、咖啡店與連鎖餐飲業者。
  • Who benefits most: small teams that manage orders manually and want fewer stock surprises.最有感的族群：目前仍靠人工下單、希望減少缺貨驚喜的小型團隊。
  • Class presentation angle: show how AI solves a real business pain point instead of a toy demo.簡報角度：展示 AI 如何解決真實商業痛點，而不是只做一個玩具示範。

Core problem and solution 核心問題與解法

The restaurant workflow: unpredictable demand, perishable goods, and profit pressure. 餐廳流程面對的是不穩定需求、易腐食材與獲利壓力。

Core system rules核心系統規則

  • Daily demand swings: multiple product types with changing customer flows.每日需求起伏：多種產品，客流也會隨時間變動。
  • Shared inventory capacity: limited warehouse and cross-product consumption.共享庫存容量：倉儲空間有限，而且不同產品會彼此競爭資源。
  • Perishable stock: expiration windows force smart replenishment.易腐庫存：有效期限會迫使我們更聰明地安排補貨。
  • Business objective: maximize service while minimizing waste and holding cost.商業目標：在提高服務率的同時，盡量降低浪費與持有成本。

Takeaways重點整理
Practical lessons for operators and product teams. 給營運團隊與產品團隊的實用啟發。

The best AI projects solve a concrete operational problem and show a measurable gain. 最好的 AI 專案，應該解決具體營運問題，並且看得見成效。

Key business lessons關鍵商業重點

  • Avoid hidden state: isolate policy changes from simulation state and cached behavior.避免隱藏狀態：把策略變更和模擬狀態、快取行為分開處理。
  • Build fallback safety: the product should still give useful suggestions when data is incomplete.建立備援機制：當資料不完整時，系統仍應給出有用建議。
  • Use measurable feedback: progress should be evident in numbers, not just slides.使用可衡量回饋：進步應該體現在數字上，而不只是投影片裡。

Scalability safeguards擴充保護

  • The architecture can be reused across branches and cities.這套架構可以在不同分店與城市之間重複使用。
  • APIs make supplier, booking, and delivery integrations possible.API 讓供應商、訂位與外送整合成為可能。
  • The multilingual interface is ready for English, Chinese, and future markets.多語言介面已可支援英文、中文與未來市場。
